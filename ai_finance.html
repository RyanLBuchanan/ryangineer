Finance Fundamentals

Data Processing

   ↳ Market data is temporal in nature → a series of trading events that will happen in a moment of time
  • Technical Indicators
    • Constant Threshold (CT)
      • CT = | price - average | > x dollars
    • Fraction Threshold (FT)
      • FT =   | price - average | price   >   x percent
  • Fundamental Information
    • Sales Per Share
      • SPS =   revenue # of shares
    • Earnings Per Share
      • EPS =   revenue - costs # of shares
    • Dividends Per Share
      • DPS =   dividends # of shares
    • Price to Earnings Ratio (PE ratio)
      • PE ratio =   current mkt price most recent EPS
  • Stock Returns
    • Returns (aka Linear, Percentage, Raw, Simple)
      • r =   p t - p t - 1 p t - 1
    • Log Return
      • R = ln ( p t p t - 1 )
    • Convert between raw returns & log returns
      • R = ln(r + 1)
      r = e R - 1
  • Rates of Compounding
    • Straightforward Yearly Interest
      Future Value   =   Deposit Amount   *   ( 1 + Annual Interest Rate )
    • Continuous Compounding
      Formula for Compounding
      p t   =   p t - 1 ( 1 + r n ) n
    • Calculus for Limit
      lim n → ∞ ( 1 +   r n ) n   =   e r
        ↳ where:
        r = continuously compounded annual return
      r = ln ( p t p t - 1 )   ⇒   log return
  • Trading Strategy
    • Mean Monthly Return
      x̄   =   1 n Σxi
        ↳ where:
        n = # of monthly returns in the sample
    • Standard Deviation
      s   =   1 n Σ ( x i   -   x̄ ) 2
    • t-Statistic
      Way of testing probability getting as large of mean with correct assumptions made to build our model of strategy returns
      t   =   x̄ SE x̄
    • Standard Error of the Mean
      SE x̄   =   s n
